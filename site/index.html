What it does

Better prompts without the back-and-forth.

One-click optimize

An Optimize button appears right in the ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude prompt box — or press Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+O. Review the rewrite in a preview, then accept it.

15+ presets

From quick grammar fixes to full structural rewrites, persuasive copy, engineering specs, and first-principles breakdowns — pick the right preset for the job.

Three AI providers

Use Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic Claude — your call. Set your API key once. No subscription, no middleman.

Phrase Better

Right-click any selected text on the web to rephrase it. Pick a preset — fix grammar, rephrase, casual reply, or formal message — and stack adjustments like keep my voice, polish wording, or add a little wit.

Usage reports

A built-in 30-day dashboard charts your requests by provider and by app. Computed and stored entirely on your device — nothing is sent anywhere, and it clears itself after 30 days.

Always-current models

The model list loads live from each provider, so new flagship models show up automatically — no extension update needed. Just verify your key and pick from the latest.

Works where you work

Injected directly into ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Claude. No copy-pasting to a separate tool. The Optimize button moves with your workflow.

Draggable & unobtrusive

The Optimize button is draggable — park it anywhere that suits your layout. It disappears when you don't need it.

Presets

A preset for every kind of prompt.

Four categories for the Optimize button — switch in one click from the popup — plus dedicated presets for the right-click Phrase Better.

Core Rewrite

Structural rewrites for clarity, brevity, grammar, and phrasing.

  • Structured — clear, flowing, high-context (scaled to your input)
  • Concise — shorter while preserving requirements
  • Fix Grammar — spelling & grammar with minimal rewrite
  • Improve Clarity — clearer phrasing, same meaning

Communication Style

Tailor the tone for your specific audience and context.

  • Persuasive — compelling and outcome-oriented
  • Executive Brief — concise, strategic, stakeholder-ready
  • Coaching Tone — supportive, accountable, action-driven
  • Email Rephrase — polished, clear email-ready rewrite

Critical Thinking

Pressure-test ideas before you act on them.

  • Devil's Advocate — challenge assumptions and blind spots
  • First Principles — decompose to fundamentals
  • Risk Audit — risks, failure modes, and mitigations

Build & Delivery

Engineering and planning precision for technical work.

  • Technical Spec — precise requirements and acceptance criteria
  • Implementation Plan — ordered tasks, dependencies, deliverables

Phrase Better

For the right-click rephrase on any page. Pick a preset, then stack optional adjustments.

  • Fix Grammar — default; smallest edits, keeps your wording
  • Rephrase — reword for clarity and flow
  • Casual Reply — relaxed, friendly tone
  • Formal Message — polished and professional
  • Adjustments — keep my voice · polish wording · add a little wit

Privacy first

Your prompts stay yours.

AskBetter has no backend. Everything runs in your browser. Your API key never touches a server we own.

No backend server

API calls go directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider. There is no intermediate server.

Local storage only

Settings, API keys, and your 30-day usage history live in chrome.storage.local on your device. Never synced to the cloud.

Key isolation

Your API key is only readable by the background service worker. Content scripts — and the pages you visit — never see it.

Zero telemetry

No analytics or crash reporting ever leaves your device. The usage Reports are computed and stored only on your machine — we never see them.
