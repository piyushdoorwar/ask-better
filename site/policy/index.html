AskBetter Chrome Extension

Privacy Policy

AskBetter is local-first. Your prompts, settings, and API keys stay in your browser. No backend server. No developer analytics. No telemetry.

Last updated: May 2026 Developer collection: None Third-party AI: Optional, user-controlled

01

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the AskBetter browser extension (the "Extension"). By installing or using AskBetter, you agree to this policy.

02

Data Collection

AskBetter does not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal data to the developer. The Extension does not require accounts, logins, or identity information.

No developer analytics. No hidden telemetry.

03

Local Data Storage

AskBetter stores limited data locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local:

  • User preferences and settings (provider, model, preset, toggles)
  • Your Gemini, OpenAI, and/or Anthropic API keys
  • Custom prompt additions
  • Draggable button position offsets

This data stays on your device. Remove it by uninstalling the Extension or clearing extension storage in your browser settings.

04

Prompts, Page Content, and Processing

AskBetter reads prompt text from ChatGPT and Google Gemini input boxes only when you trigger an Optimize action. Text is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider. AskBetter does not route content through any developer-controlled server.

The developer does not access, store, or monitor your prompts, page selections, or AI outputs.

05

API Keys and Credentials

API keys you enter are stored only in chrome.storage.local on your device. They are accessible only to the extension's background service worker — content scripts and web pages never read them. The developer never receives your keys.

You are responsible for managing and rotating your keys safely with the relevant provider.

06

Third-Party Services

Third-party AI providers (Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic) are optional and user-configured. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies and terms. The developer is not responsible for third-party data practices.

Connecting an external provider means your prompt is sent to that provider's servers.

07

Permissions

AskBetter requests these browser permissions strictly to enable core features:

  • storage — saves your settings and API keys locally.
  • activeTab / scripting — injects the Optimize button and reads/writes the prompt input on supported pages when triggered.
  • contextMenus — adds the "Re-phrase with AskBetter" right-click action.
  • host_permissions — makes direct API calls to Gemini, OpenAI, and Anthropic endpoints from the background worker.

No permissions are used for tracking or advertising.

08

Analytics and Tracking

AskBetter does not use analytics or tracking tools and does not profile users. No usage events, crash reports, or interaction data are sent anywhere.

09

Data Security

All data handled by AskBetter remains within the browser environment. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

10

Children's Privacy

AskBetter does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.
